Cultural Heritage of Jharkhand

Rich traditions & vibrant communities
Indigenous Heritage

Tribal Communities

Home to 32+ indigenous tribes including Santhal, Munda, Oraon, Ho, and Kharia communities.

  • Traditional dance forms: Chhau, Karam, Jhumair
  • Ancient folk music & instruments
  • Colorful festivals & rituals
  • Traditional attire & jewelry
Artisan Crafts

Traditional Handicrafts

Exquisite craftsmanship passed down through generations.

  • Dokra metal casting art
  • Bamboo & wood crafts
  • Traditional pottery
  • Paitkar painting & Sohrai art
  • Tassar silk weaving
Celebrations

Cultural Festivals

Vibrant celebrations that showcase Jharkhand's rich cultural tapestry.

  • Sarhul - Spring festival
  • Karam - Harvest festival
  • Tusu Parab - Folk celebration
  • Holi & Diwali with tribal flavors
  • Local village fairs & markets
